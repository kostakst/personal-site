Composite Stacking Optimization Tool

A practical engineering tool for evaluating composite stacking alternatives with design rules, manufacturability constraints, and laminate logic in view.

Domain
Composite structures
Methods
Stacking logic, constraints, optimization workflow
Status
Tool summary

Problem

Composite stacking decisions require balancing laminate performance, ply continuity, symmetry, manufacturing limits, and repeated engineering checks across many alternatives.

Approach

The tool structures these inputs into a repeatable workflow so candidate stacking sequences can be compared with clearer constraint visibility and less manual iteration.
