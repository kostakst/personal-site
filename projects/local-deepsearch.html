Study summary

Overview

These studies focus on practical local AI inside an engineering company context: keeping data local, making internal knowledge easier to reach, and testing where LocalLLM workflows can support day-to-day design work.

Study Areas

LocalLLM Chat Interface A local chat interface for experimenting with model behavior, prompts, and engineering assistant workflows without relying on cloud inference.
LocalLLM RAG System A retrieval-augmented system using company documents so engineers can query internal technical material through a controlled local workflow.
Embedding Image Search An embedding-based image search engine for finding visually or semantically related engineering images and references more efficiently.
3DEXPERIENCE Checker Support LocalLLM-supported checks for selected 3DEXPERIENCE design-checker tasks, helping evaluate where language models can assist repetitive review steps.

Why It Matters

Technical organizations lose time when reports, standards, drawings, images, and engineering references are hard to search across. Local LLM and embedding workflows can improve discovery while keeping sensitive company material inside controlled environments.
