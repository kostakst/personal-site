Overview

The studies focus on practical local AI workflows: ingesting technical documents, preserving local control, and helping engineers find relevant information faster.

Why It Matters

Technical organizations lose time when reports, standards, drawings, and references are searchable only by filename. Local LLM workflows can improve discovery while keeping sensitive material away from cloud services.
