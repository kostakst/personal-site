Mechanical Design / Machine Learning Tool

Alternative Fastener Selection Tool

A machine-learning-supported tool I developed to reduce repetitive fastener selection work while keeping mechanical constraints and engineering rules visible.

Domain
Mechanical design automation
Methods
Machine learning, Python, design rules, selection logic
Status
Tool summary

Problem

Fastener selection becomes slow when geometry, design rules, standards, availability, and engineering preferences must be reconciled across many similar cases.

Approach

I developed the tool to structure inputs, constraints, and ranking logic, then use machine learning support to help prioritize candidate fasteners for comparison.

Confidentiality

Tool screenshots, internal selection data, and company-specific rules cannot be shared publicly. The page describes the purpose and engineering logic at a high level.

What can be shared

The tool reflects work in mechanical design automation, machine-learning-supported candidate ranking, constraint-based filtering, engineering rule application, and faster comparison of fastener alternatives.
